About Us

Cued In Research was started by Dipanyita Ray in 2018 in Chennai

Dipanyita moved from a large well established research agency to set up Cued In Research specially for clients with high research needs and interest but small research budgets.

She felt these clients with limited research budgets were often by passed by the big research suppliers, though their needs were substantial.

Cued In Research was set up for Clients like you. Clients who understand and value authentic consumer insights But are limited by time and budget constraints.
